HERE'S A LIST OF HTML TAGS I KNOW

  • head - a container for information that is not the content of your site
  • body - a container for the site content
  • meta - used to store additional information like the charset
  • h1 - the largest header. The other headers are h2, h3, h4, h5, and h6.
  • p - the paragraph element
  • ul - the unordered list element. There is also ol, for ordered lists.
  • li - an element in a list (like this one)
  • title - sets the site title, which appears in the tab of the browser
  • strong - to make a word bold
  • em - to make a word italicized
